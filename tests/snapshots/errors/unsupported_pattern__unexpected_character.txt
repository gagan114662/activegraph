UnsupportedPatternError: pattern does not parse: unexpected character at position 10

What failed:
  While parsing the pattern: unexpected character at position 10.
    at: '@'

Why:
  Behaviors register their pattern subscriptions at startup, so the parser refuses ambiguous syntax now rather than risk matching a pattern the developer did not actually write. An unparseable pattern is a configuration bug; matching is the next concern.

How to fix:
  Fix the syntax. The supported subset is documented in CONTRACT v0.7 #8 and at
      https://yoheinakajima.github.io/activegraph/concepts/patterns
  If the syntax looks right, check for unbalanced brackets, a missing relationship type, or a missing arrow direction.
