UnsupportedPatternError: OR is not supported in the v0.7 Cypher subset

What failed:
  The pattern uses OR. The v0.7 subset refuses this feature at registration time — long before any match runs.

Why:
  OR in WHERE clauses can produce match-set ambiguity at the trace level: it's hard to tell, after the fact, which branch of the OR actually triggered. Registering two behaviors keeps every fire attributable to a specific pattern in the audit trail. See CONTRACT v0.7 #8.

How to fix:
  Register two behaviors, one per branch of the disjunction.
  Both fire independently; if both branches are true for the
  same event, both behaviors fire (which is usually what you
  want — OR-then-dedup is not).

  Example:
    Instead of: WHERE c.confidence > 0.7 OR c.severity = 'high'
    Register:   one behavior with WHERE c.confidence > 0.7
                one behavior with WHERE c.severity = 'high'
